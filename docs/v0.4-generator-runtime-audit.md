## Solar Panel

`SolarPanel.EnergySim200ms(float dt)` computes light-based wattage, clamps it to 0-380 W, accumulates the actual rate, and exposes:

```text
SolarPanel.CurrentWattage
```

0.4.3 uses this value for:

```text
SolarHeatDtuPerSecond = clamp(CurrentWattage / 380, 0, 1) * 5000
EnergyDtu = SolarHeatDtuPerSecond * dt
```

The implementation does not inspect sunlight, Shine Bugs, lamps, or cells around the panel.

| `MethaneGenerator` | 800 W | 20 kDTU/s |
| `PetroleumGenerator` | 2000 W | 50 kDTU/s |

Still not active:

- fuel temperature capture;
- product output temperature correction;
- distribution of excess fuel heat to outputs/body;
- generator tooltip ledger.

Fuel thermal accounting needs a separate prototype because safe fuel-consumption and product-emission hooks have not yet been proven for every generator class.
