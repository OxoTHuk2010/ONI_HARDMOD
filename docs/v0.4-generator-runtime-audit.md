## Runtime Points

Confirmed:

```text
Generator.WattageRating
Generator.BaseWattageRating
Generator.GenerateJoules(float, bool)
Generator.EnergySim200ms(float)
SolarPanel.EnergySim200ms(float)
SolarPanel.CurrentWattage
StructureTemperaturePayload.OperatingKilowatts
StructureTemperaturePayload.ExhaustKilowatts
SimMessages.ModifyBuildingEnergy(int, float, float, float)
```

0.4.5 uses `Generator.WattageRating` only for the explicit v0.4 profile table. The older percentage-based 0.3 generator-efficiency mechanism was removed from active source and configuration.

`StructureTemperatureComponents` registers native building heat through `SimMessages.AddBuildingHeatExchange` using `StructureTemperaturePayload.OperatingKilowatts`. In-game testing showed that changing descriptors/getters did not reliably produce the intended body heat on existing generator instances. 0.4.5 therefore sets profiled generator `OperatingKilowatts` to zero and injects profile heat directly during:

```text
EnergyGenerator.EnergySim200ms(float)
EnergyGenerator.Emit(OutputItem,float,PrimaryElement)
ManualGenerator.EnergySim200ms(float)
```

The direct injection uses:

```text
EnergyDtu = BodyHeatKilowatts * 1000 * dt
SimMessages.ModifyBuildingEnergy(...)
```

For profiled `EnergyGenerator` buildings with fuel-temperature accounting enabled in their profile, the prefix captures stored fuel mass and temperature before vanilla consumption, and the postfix compares remaining mass after vanilla consumption. Any consumed hot fuel contributes:

```text
FuelSurplusDtu =
    ConsumedMassKg * 1000 * FuelSHC * max(0, FuelTemperatureK - BodyTemperatureK)
```

The surplus is added to products and the body through the same thermal accounting path. Product mass and product type are not changed. Since 0.4.7, direct gas output minimum temperature is raised to the captured hot fuel temperature; stored gas products are corrected in generator storage after `Emit(...)`; liquid output temperature is capped at `Element.highTemp - 1 K`; heat assigned to outputs is subtracted from the body surplus to avoid double counting.

## Solar Panel

`SolarPanel.EnergySim200ms(float dt)` computes light-based wattage, clamps it to 0-380 W, accumulates the actual rate, and exposes:

```text
SolarPanel.CurrentWattage
```

0.4.3 uses this value for:

```text
SolarHeatDtuPerSecond = clamp(CurrentWattage / 380, 0, 1) * 5000
EnergyDtu = SolarHeatDtuPerSecond * dt
```

The implementation does not inspect sunlight, Shine Bugs, lamps, or cells around the panel.

## Generator Rebalance Status

Active in 0.4.4:

| Prefab ID | Wattage | Body heat |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| `ManualGenerator` | 400 W | 1 kDTU/s |
| `WoodGasGenerator` | 300 W | 20 kDTU/s |
| `Generator` | 600 W | 40 kDTU/s |
| `PeatGenerator` | 480 W | 22.5 kDTU/s |
| `HydrogenGenerator` | 800 W | 32 kDTU/s |
| `MethaneGenerator` | 800 W | 20 kDTU/s |
| `PetroleumGenerator` | 2000 W | 50 kDTU/s |

Still not active:

- fuel temperature capture;
- product output temperature correction;
- distribution of excess fuel heat to outputs/body;
- generator tooltip ledger.

Stored-output paths are handled by correcting matching product elements in generator storage after `Emit(...)`, but still need in-game verification for each generator config. Hot hydrogen, hot natural gas, and hot petroleum add surplus heat to the body; direct and stored gas outputs can inherit hot fuel temperature; liquid outputs are capped below phase transition.
